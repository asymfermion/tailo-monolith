Your pet's week, remembered for you.

Tailo looks through recent dog and cat photos on your device, picks out a few likely moments, and turns them into a timeline you did not have to sort.

Built for the sofa photos, sunny naps, blurry zoomies, and tiny habits that usually disappear into the camera roll.

It is not another photo grid.

The first version is intentionally small: recent photos, one pet, dogs and cats, and a timeline that gets useful before it gets fancy.

01

Tailo checks recent photos on your device.

Start with the last few weeks. You can keep using the app while it looks for likely pet moments.

02

It keeps the useful few, not every similar shot.

A nap might have twenty photos. Tailo tries to save the two or three that actually tell the story.

03

You get moments, grouped by time.

“Morning window watch” lands as one memory, not a stack of files you need to clean up later.

Your camera roll is not the product.

Tailo starts on-device and never asks you to upload your whole library. Only selected moments are saved.

For people with too many pet photos and no time to sort them.
